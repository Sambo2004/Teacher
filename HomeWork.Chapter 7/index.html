We welcome newcomers who share AUB's vision and mission. Yes, we believe in creating maximum economic value for our students and shareholders, but not at the expense of human values and principles.

Whether you're aspiring to the first rung of the career ladder or you're an experienced professional, we can introduce you here to the many opportunities available throughout ACLEDA Bank's largest nationwide branch network in the Kingdom of Cambodia. We provide Master/Bachelor/Associate degree program majoring specifically in Finance and Banking, Supply Chain Management and Logistics, Risk Management and Insurance, International Business, Fintech, and Business Information Technologies.
